Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

G'ma's favorite thing for me to do with chicken thighs. This is the Instant pot version and it rocks. Serve it with rice, roasted potatoes, naan, whatever it goes great with anything.

ingredients

Chicken Marinade:

  • One pound of boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into bite sized chunks
  • a cup of plain greek yogurt
  • 1 whole fresh lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of Garam Masala seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of cracked black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • A Teeny Tiny pinch of cayanne pepper

Sauce:

  • 15 Oz. can of Tomato Sauce
  • 5 cloves of fresh minced garlic
  • 4 teaspoons of Garam Masala seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cayenne pepper (less for G'Ma)
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream

Garnish:

  • Cut the other half of the lemon into wedges
  • freshly chopped parsly or cilantro (either is fine as long as fresh)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the lemon in half. Squeeze half a lemon into a bowl
  2. Mix the rest of the marinade ingredients well into the bowl
  3. Mix the cut chicken into the marinade, coating every piece
  4. Cover the bowl and put in the fridge for at least an hour and up to overnight to... you know... Marinate
  5. Saute the chicken on med-high heat until seared on all sides
  6. Deglaze the pan/InstaPot to remove the fond
  7. Put the chicken and All of the sauce ingredients EXCEPT THE HEAVY CREAM! into the InstantPot
  8. Stir the whole mess together well and put the lid on
  9. Seal the lid and cook at high pressure for 10 minutes
  10. Do a quick release and vent the pressure from the pot
  11. Immediately add the heavy whipping cream and stir until creamy and smooth
  12. Garnish with parsly or cilantro and lemon wedges
