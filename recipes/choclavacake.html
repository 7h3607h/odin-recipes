ingredients

The Batter

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1.5 teaspoons of vanilla extract

The Topping

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa powder

1 and a 1/3 cup HOT water

Instructions:

  1. Start off by pre-heating the oven to 350o Farenheit.
  2. grease up a 9x9 pan
  3. Mix up all the ingredients in the batter section, then pour into the greased 9x9 pan
  4. Mix up the topping then sprinkle it evenly over the batter
  5. Pour the hot water over the topping
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
