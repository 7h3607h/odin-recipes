Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is the blah blah blah descriptive part of the recipe

ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) softened butter
  • 2 cups chocolate (chips/chunks/whatever)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions:

  1. Start off by pre-heating the oven to 350o Farenheit.
  2. set out cookie sheets and cooling racks
  3. in a large bowl cream the sugar and brown sugar together with the eggs
  4. Add the remining ingredients and mix well until doughy
  5. Drop tablespoon sized clumps of dough on cookie sheets
  6. bake for 10 minutes.
  7. once baked, remove from baking sheet and cool on a rack or plate
  8. rinse and repeat steps 5 thru 8 until out of dough
